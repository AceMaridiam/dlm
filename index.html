About Me

My name is Paul Anderson. I also go by Ace.
I've been working with video for over a decade. I've written, shot, edited, composited and done sound design for many projects. I help produce a local web series, "The Freebox" as well as a few films. I love doing video produciton, especially comedy skits.

I got into web design out of curiousity; seeing the direction the web was heading I figured I could combine my skills. I now have about 5 years of web design and development under my belt; my focus going towards visually interesting sites with engaging interactive content.

I also practice Parkour and I'm a firm believer in pushing human potential.

Contact
